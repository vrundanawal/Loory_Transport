Welcome to Neely Transport

We are a well established General Haulage operation based in the Heart of the West Midlands, near the N.E.C. and Birmingham airport.

We offer Groupage, part load and full load services throughout the UK and as members of The Pallet Network (TPN ) we can offer nationwide coverage for almost any type of freight on a 24/48 hour basis. Furthermore, we have an independently appointed D.G.S.A. on site for the purpose of carriage of Hazardous goods and many of our drivers are trained for this purpose.

EMERGENCY SAMEDAY DELIVERY

Our diverse fleet of vehicles are available at short notice, for that emergency delivery situation. Our business was built on our ability to provide a fast and reliable response, night or day, 7 days a week.

PALLET NETWORK

UPN network member offering palletised freight delivery from micro pallets upwards, throughout UK and Europe.

Warehouse Storage

The new Actros from Mercedes-Benz is available from our TruckStore network. The Actros wrote history and to this day is the flagship of Mercedes-Benz Trucks in the heavy-duty class.

Additional Services

KBTS can also offer tailor-made services for you needs, which include same-day express deliveries for your requirements. Door-door European and international services.

About Us

The final, and most recent change, has been the steady growth of Pallet Networks. Neely Transport joined “The Pallet Network” (TPN) in 2008 and whilst the carriage of palletised freight is not our core business it offers significant advantages in time, cost and service for many items. Furthermore, it means we can truly offer a 24/48 hour delivery schedule throughout the UK in the sound knowledge that our partners in TPN will treat every kilo of freight as if they were servicing one of their own accounts.
